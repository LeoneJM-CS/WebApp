The goal of this soccer club is to show the love of Christ through integrity, sportsmanship, encouragement, respect, and fun.

We exist to develop skilled, disciplined soccer players who compete with excellence while reflecting the character of Christ on and off the field.

1 Thessalonians 5:11 Therefore encourage one another and build each other up, just as in fact you are doing.

1 Thessalonians 5:18 Give thanks in all circumstances; for this is God's will for you in Christ Jesus.

Colorado Vanguard FC is a Christ-centered soccer program built for players who want more than a typical team experience. We combine high-level training with intentional mentorship, creating an environment where athletes grow in both ability and character.

Founded with a vision to elevate youth soccer in Colorado, Vanguard emphasizes teamwork, accountability, and a culture that honors God in everything we do. Whether in training, competition, or community, we strive to model integrity and purpose.

Our identity as a Christian club isn't a slogan — it's woven into our rhythm:
  • Team prayer before and after games
  • Short devotionals and character talks
  • Service opportunities within the community
  • Expectations that reflect Christlike conduct
  • Coaches who mentor both the athlete and the person
We compete hard, but we compete differently — with humility, respect, and purpose.
Whether you're an experienced player or new to the sport, we invite you to be part of a team that plays with passion, purpose, and faith. At Colorado Vanguard FC, you'll find a place to grow, compete, and belong.
